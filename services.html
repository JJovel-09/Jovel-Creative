Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most websites are completed within 2-4 weeks, depending on the package selected and the complexity of your requirements. Starter packages typically take 2-3 weeks, while more complex projects may take 3-4 weeks to ensure everything is perfect.

Do I own my website?

Yes, absolutely! You own all the content, design, and code for your website. You can take it anywhere, host it with any provider, or work with any developer you choose. There are no proprietary systems or lock-in contracts.

What's included in your services?

Services include brand strategy, visual identity design, website development, content strategy, and ongoing support. Each project is tailored to your specific needs, and we work with you one-on-one throughout the entire process.

Do you build e-commerce sites?

Yes! We can integrate payment processing (Stripe, PayPal, Square), product catalogs, shopping carts, inventory management, and order tracking based on your needs. Perfect for retail businesses or service businesses selling products.

What areas do you serve?

We serve small businesses and organizations throughout Frederick County and Montgomery County, Maryland, including Frederick, Urbana, Mount Airy, Rockville, Gaithersburg, and surrounding areas. We also work remotely with clients throughout Maryland and beyond.
