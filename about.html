Why I Don't Use WordPress

You might wonder why I build custom websites instead of using WordPress like most developers. The answer is simple: I want to give you the best possible website, not the easiest one for me to build.

Custom-coded websites are faster, more secure, easier to maintain, and can be precisely tailored to your needs without the bloat and complexity of WordPress. Your business deserves a website built specifically for you, not forced into a one-size-fits-all template.

Why Custom Code Instead of WordPress?

Here's what you gain with a custom-built website from Jovel Creative:

Speed

No bloated plugins slowing things down. Custom websites typically load 2-3x faster than WordPress sites. Faster sites rank higher in Google and convert more visitors into customers.

Security

WordPress powers 43% of the web—making it a huge target for hackers. Custom code means no WordPress vulnerabilities, no plugin security holes, and a much smaller attack surface.

Customization

Built exactly for your needs, not forced into templates. Every element serves a purpose for your business. No compromises, no "the template won't let me do that."

Maintenance

Simpler, cleaner code means fewer things to break. No plugin conflicts, no "update broke the site" emergencies. Updates are straightforward and predictable.

Performance

Optimized from the ground up for your specific use case. Every line of code serves a purpose. Better performance means better SEO, better user experience, and better conversions.

Total Cost of Ownership

No expensive premium plugins or themes. No surprise "your plugin license expired" fees. Lower long-term maintenance costs. You own everything—code, design, content.

My Web Development Process

A proven, transparent process that ensures you get exactly what you need—on time and on budget.

1

Discovery - Understanding Your Business

We start with a conversation about your business, goals, target audience, and what success looks like for you. I'll ask questions about your competitors, what you love about other websites, and what features are must-haves vs. nice-to-haves. This ensures I understand exactly what you need before any work begins.

2

Design - Creating Mockups for Your Approval

I'll create visual mockups of your homepage and key interior pages. This gives you a clear picture of how your site will look before any code is written. We'll collaborate on colors, layout, imagery, and overall design until you're completely satisfied. No surprises.

3

Development - Building Your Site with Clean Code

With approved designs in hand, I'll build your website using clean, optimized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Every page is built with SEO in mind, optimized for speed, and tested on multiple devices and browsers. I'll provide you with a staging link so you can review progress along the way.

4

Launch - Testing, Deployment, and Training

Before launch, I thoroughly test your site for performance, mobile responsiveness, browser compatibility, and functionality. Once you give final approval, I'll deploy your site to your live domain, set up analytics, and provide training on how to request updates or manage content (if you chose a package with admin panel access).

5

Support - Ongoing Maintenance and Updates

Your website isn't "finished" at launch—it's a living tool for your business. I provide reliable hosting, security monitoring, backups, and updates as needed. Whether you choose self-service content updates or hands-off managed support, I'm here to ensure your site continues to perform and grow with your business.
