# Local SEO Guide for Frederick & Montgomery County

Comprehensive guide to dominating local search results for "web developer Frederick MD" and related keywords.

## Why Local SEO Matters

- 46% of all Google searches have local intent
- 76% of people who search for something nearby visit a business within 24 hours
- 28% of local searches result in a purchase
- **You're competing locally, not nationally**

## Step 2: Get Reviews (Critical for Rankings!)

### Review Strategy

**Goal**: Get to 10+ reviews in first 6 months, then 2-3 new reviews per month.

**How to Get Reviews:**

1. **After project completion**, send this email:

```
Subject: Thank you for choosing Jovel Creative!

Hi [Name],

Thank you for trusting Jovel Creative with your website project. I hope you're thrilled with the results!

If you're happy with our work, I'd greatly appreciate if you could share your experience on Google. Reviews help other Frederick businesses find us.

Leave a review here: [Your Google Review Link]

It takes just 2 minutes and means the world to a small business like mine.

Thank you again!

Juan Jovel
Jovel Creative
240-579-7858
```

2. **Find Your Review Link:**
   - Go to Google Business Profile Manager
   - Click "Home"
   - Find "Get more reviews" link
   - Copy and save this link
   - Example: `https://g.page/r/[YOUR_CODE]/review`

3. **Make It Easy:**
   - Add review link to email signature
   - Include on invoices
   - Create a short link: bit.ly/jovelcreative-review

**Responding to Reviews:**

**For 5-Star Reviews:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! It was a pleasure working with you on your website. I'm glad we could help [mention specific result]. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you need anything!

- Juan
```

**For 4-Star Reviews:**
```
Thank you for your review, [Name]! I appreciate your feedback on [mention positive]. I'd love to hear more about what could have made it a 5-star experience. Please reach out directly so I can make it right!

- Juan
```

**For Negative Reviews:**
```
Hi [Name], I'm sorry to hear about your experience. This isn't the level of service we strive for. Please contact me directly at juan@jovelcreative.com so I can understand what happened and make it right.

- Juan
```

**Golden Rule**: Respond to EVERY review within 24 hours, even if it's just "Thank you!"

## Common Local SEO Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Inconsistent NAP across directories
- ❌ Not claiming Google Business Profile
- ❌ Ignoring reviews or not responding
- ❌ Using a PO Box instead of physical address
- ❌ Keyword stuffing location names
- ❌ Not optimizing for "near me" searches
- ❌ Having duplicate listings
- ❌ Not tracking local rankings
